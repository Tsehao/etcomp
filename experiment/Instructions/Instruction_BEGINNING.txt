Welcome to our experiment. \n
This experiment consists of 10 small tasks that will repeat six time. Before each condition you will you will be shown instructions to your task. \n
At the beginning of the experiment and after each block, we will calibrate the eytracker. At each instruction screen you can take a break for as long as you want to. \n
\n

Whenever you see a fixation cross, it is important that you look at it and don't move your eyes away.\n

\n
The experiment will take about 35 minutes to complete. \n
Please let us know if there are any open questions. Otherwise press SPACE to start.
